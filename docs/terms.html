Terms of Use

Last updated: 2026-05-24

Free Usage

FileSort offers a free tier with monthly usage limits:

  • 3 file sorts per calendar month — resets at the start of each month
  • 1 duplicate scan per calendar month — resets at the start of each month
  • 1 custom sort rule
  • 1 undo batch in history

Free usage counts are stored locally on your device and reset automatically each month.

Subscription Plans

FileSort offers the following paid plans:

  • Monthly Subscription — $3.99/month
  • Yearly Subscription — $19.99/year (save 58%)
  • Lifetime Purchase — $39.99 one-time payment

All paid plans unlock the same premium features with no usage limits.

Auto-Renewal

Monthly and yearly subscriptions automatically renew unless cancelled at least 24 hours before the end of the current period. Your account will be charged for renewal within 24 hours prior to the end of the current period.

Cancellation

You can cancel your subscription anytime through your Apple ID settings:

  1. Open the Settings app on your iPhone/iPad
  2. Tap your name at the top
  3. Tap "Subscriptions"
  4. Find FileSort and tap "Cancel Subscription"

Or visit: https://apps.apple.com/account/subscriptions

Lifetime Purchase

The Lifetime Purchase is a one-time payment that grants permanent access to all premium features. It does not require renewal and does not auto-renew.

What Happens When a Subscription Ends

If you cancel a subscription, you will retain premium features until the end of your current billing period. After that, you will revert to the free tier with monthly usage limits described above.

Refund Policy

All purchases are processed through the App Store. For refund requests, please contact Apple Support at https://support.apple.com/billing.

Restore Purchases

You can restore previous purchases on any device by going to Settings > Restore Purchases within the app.
